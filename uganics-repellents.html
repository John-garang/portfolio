About Uganics Repellents Ltd

Uganics Repellents Ltd, founded in 2017, is a Ugandan social enterprise producing organic mosquito-repellent soap to combat malaria. Their mission is to provide eco-friendly malaria prevention solutions by subsidizing costs for vulnerable communities through sales to tourists.

Uganics has impacted over 3 million lives and empowers women by creating jobs in soap production and distribution, thereby promoting economic independence. Their efforts contribute to global health initiatives while fostering women's empowerment and local economic growth.
