About UNLEASH

UNLEASH is a nonprofit powerhouse on a mission to accelerate progress toward the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by mobilizing young changemakers from around the world. They gather top talents (18â€“35 years old) for immersive Innovation Labs and regional programs, faster than think tanks, more dynamic than lectures, where diverse youth co-create scalable, real-world solutions.

Since the first lab in Copenhagen in 2017, UNLEASH has held global and regional Innovation Labs, from Singapore and China to Greenland and Rwanda, engaged thousands of young leaders, and launched hundreds of impactful SDG-focused projects.

Kigali Innovation Lab Experience

In December 2023, I joined the UNLEASH Global Innovation Lab in Kigali, Rwanda, as one of 1,000 young talents selected from around the world. I was placed in the Climate and Land track, where I collaborated with an incredible team from Colombia, The Gambia, the United States, Pakistan, and Iceland.

Together, we developed PappyBags, a sustainable solution to replace single-use plastic bags in South Sudan by using locally abundant papyrus reeds to create reusable tote bags. Our innovation aimed to address both environmental pollution and economic empowerment, turning an underutilized natural resource into a source of income for communities.
