About CNN Academy

CNN Academy equips the next generation of journalists with real-world skills through immersive, newsroom-style learning. Drawing on CNN's legacy, it brings newsroom expertise into classrooms—from Dublin to Kuala Lumpur—connecting students with top CNN correspondents and transforming storytelling into action.

Their offerings include everything from the Academy Hub (self-paced e-learning on journalism fundamentals, ethics, and AI), to simulation training, co-branded master's programs, and workshops rooted in multimedia reporting and climate storytelling from the Global South. With programming ranging from hands-on simulations to global bootcamps and masterclasses, including climate-focused storytelling backed by the Rockefeller Foundation, CNN Academy gives aspiring journalists the tools to navigate and shape today's complex media landscape.

Fellowship Experience

As a CNN Academy Global South Fellow, I spent months immersed in an intensive program designed to sharpen both my technical and editorial skills. The fellowship was a unique blend of online courses, expert-led masterclasses, and peer-to-peer collaboration, all focused on harnessing storytelling to address climate change from a Global South perspective.

I learned directly from seasoned CNN journalists and industry experts, covering areas such as climate science basics, investigative techniques, multimedia production, and the ethics of climate reporting. The program also challenged us to think critically about representation, ensuring that stories from our regions are told with accuracy, dignity, and urgency.
