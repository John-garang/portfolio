About Education Bridge

Education Bridge is a non-profit organization founded in 2013 with a clear purpose: to provide young people in South Sudan with access to quality education and leadership development.

The organization works with students, educators, and communities across multiple regions, building an ecosystem that supports future leaders. Leaders who can drive peace, stability, and progress in their communities.

Role Evolution: From Research to Communications

At Education Bridge, I began as a Junior Researcher, working to address one of South Sudan's greatest challenges: ensuring more young people successfully transition from secondary school to university despite difficult economic realities.

Research Phase

My early work involved exploring and documenting post-secondary transition mechanisms that could expand access to higher education for South Sudanese students. I investigated strategies that schools could adopt to prepare students academically, personally, and professionally for competitive scholarships and university admissions.

Communications Leadership

As my role evolved, I transitioned into Communications Lead, where I now focus on amplifying Education Bridge's impact through strategic storytelling, stakeholder engagement, and brand positioning. I create compelling narratives that showcase our students' journeys, highlight organizational milestones, and engage both local and international audiences.
